Thursday the 9th of June 2022 - While many people believe the urban legend it is in fact not true the Terminator franchise has at least one hidden bear in the background of each of the 6 films. While it has been highly debated that there is a black bear hidden in the diner scene of Terminator Salvation this has since been confirmed by director McG that it was just in fact a large cast member wearing a leather jacket.
