Tuesday the 14th of June 2022 - Bears are very musical animals! It has been observed in the wild Bears will experiment with different items to create crude types of rhythm instruments, they will also show curiosity and great interest in music they overhear humans enjoying especially Ska and EDM. Bears show an staggering level of talent at electric guitar and classical piano.
