Thank you for subscribing to The Bear Facts newsletter, where there's a bear there's a fact! Please look forward to enriching your day with the knowledge of life's sweetest bear necessities.

Sunday the 12th of June 2022 - The diet of Bears is much disputed thanks to wide spread misinformation of previously reputable sources Paddington Bear and Winnie The Pooh. However this was debunked in a 2002 case study where a large and varied cross section of bear representatives were taken out to dinner a strong majority indeed ordered the soup and salad option over honey and marmalade sandwiches.
